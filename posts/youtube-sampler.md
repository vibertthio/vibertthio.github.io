YouTube Sampler is a Chrome extension for turning YouTube moments into samples.

The idea was to make sampling feel immediate: stay inside the browser, find a moment, and turn it into something playable without breaking the flow.

It lets you set custom cue points for YouTube shortcuts. YouTube already maps numeric keys to positions in the video; YouTube Sampler lets you customize those points, fine-tune them with the shift key, save changes automatically in the browser, and share cue points with friends.
