Sornting = sort + song.

It is a game based on a musical machine learning algorithm that interpolates between melodies. The player listens to short musical fragments and sorts them into the right order to complete the interpolation.

The game uses Tone.js and MusicVAE. Drag the melodies into the golden box, click boxes to listen, and try to reconstruct the path between musical ideas.

- [Learn more about the model](https://magenta.tensorflow.org/music-vae)
