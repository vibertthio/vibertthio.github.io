![Étude performance still](/projects/etude-01.jpg)

Étude was built around a goal: bring back some of the colorful youth and future that victims of an incident had lost, through music, art, and technology.

The project tried to change how rehabilitation felt. The people involved were just starting to figure out their futures, and the process was emotionally painful. Adding music and creative expression was a way to bring a more positive atmosphere into that process. The whole process was recorded so it could be repeated and shared with society.
