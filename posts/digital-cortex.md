![Digital Cortex installation still](/projects/digital-cortex.png)

Digital Cortex is a generative audio-visual work about memory and how recordings transform it.

The piece imagines a DCR-TRV30 digital camcorder replaying old black digital cassettes. The original images are gone: only glowing, corrupted frames remain. The machine starts to interpret this failure as a different story, one produced by its own hardware, format, and memory.

It was shown as part of the Chromesthesia exhibition.
